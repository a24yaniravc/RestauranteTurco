Welcome to Topkapi Tazelik

In the heart of Santiago, Topkapi Tazelik brings the flavors of Turkey to your table. Inspired by the historic kitchens of the Topkapi Palace, our menu celebrates fresh ingredients, rich spices, and timeless recipes. From sizzling kebabs to house-made baklava, every dish is crafted to honor tradition while delighting modern tastes. Step inside and experience the warmth, aroma, and spirit of Istanbul in every bite.
